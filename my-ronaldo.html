History

Cristiano Ronaldo, born in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, is a global icon of football. His journey began with the local team, Andorinha, before moving to Nacional and later to Sporting CP, where his skills began to shine.

His breakthrough came in 2003, when Sir Alex Ferguson, impressed by Ronaldo’s performance against Manchester United, signed him. This marked the start of an illustrious journey at Manchester United, followed by Real Madrid, Juventus, and a return to Manchester United.

Statistics

Cristiano Ronaldo has played in over 1,150 matches across club and international competitions, scoring more than 850 goals and providing over 250 assists. His incredible consistency has led to an average of 0.74 goals per match throughout his career.

His international career with Portugal has seen him play over 200 times, making him one of the most capped players in history, with over 125 goals to his name—holding the record for the most international goals by a European player.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many goals has Cristiano Ronaldo scored in the Champions League?

As of 2024, Ronaldo has scored over 140 goals in the UEFA Champions League, making him the competition's all-time top scorer.

What is Cristiano Ronaldo's greatest achievement?

Ronaldo's greatest achievement is arguably his ability to succeed across different leagues, winning major titles in the Premier League, La Liga, and Serie A, as well as his leadership role in Portugal's Euro 2016 victory.
